# Pressure-Test Novelty

## When to use

Use whenever a novel idea, experiment, or structural proposal enters the
supervisor idea ledger or active focus queue.

## What it does

- reframes the idea as a governance input
- identifies which structures it would disturb
- makes upside, cost, and risk explicit
- recommends adopt, sandbox, defer, or reject

## Invocation

Use over idea records plus the relevant policy and trace artifacts.

Required output shape:

- one-sentence idea framing
- disturbed structures
- expected upside
- complexity/risk cost
- smallest valid test surface
- disposition

## Agent notes

- Claude Code: push back with intelligent narrowing, not refusal posture.
- Codex: make the structural cost concrete and artifact-linked.
